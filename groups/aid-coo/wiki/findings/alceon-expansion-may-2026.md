- **Status as of 2026-04-07**: Confirmed. Commercials and team TBD.

## Open Questions / Considerations

**Resourcing**
- 5 staggered use cases = team needs to ramp progressively over ~5 weeks. Who's available from mid-May?
- Does the existing retainer team (IS, JD, AB) carry over, or is this a fresh build?
- What's the overlap window between retainer end (18 May) and new engagement start?

**Commercials**
- Current retainer day rate is $1,073 — the lowest in the portfolio. Is this new engagement priced differently given it's project-based (not retainer)?

**Delivery**

- Are the 5 subsidiary companies aligned on what they want, or will scoping be part of the engagement?

**Relationship**
- Alceon asking for an expanded team is a strong signal of satisfaction with current delivery.
- Opportunity to reprice / reframe away from the low retainer rate.
