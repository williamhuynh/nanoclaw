# Bound Interactive — Ongoing Partnership

## The Finding
**AiD has an ongoing strategic partnership with [Bound Interactive](../entities/bound-interactive.md).** Confirmed by Will 2026-04-16: *"Bound partnerships is a thing and will continue to be."*

This is not a one-off joint pitch — it's a pattern that should be assumed across the pipeline where the engagement profile fits.

## Why It Works (Structural Fit)
- **Bound**: CX/UX, design, mobile development. Not AI-native.
- **AiD**: AI strategy, governance, delivery. Not design/build-native.
- Complementary, not competitive. Clients needing both an experience play AND an AI play can be served jointly, with neither firm needing to extend beyond their core.

## Implications for AiD
- **Pipeline screening**: When a client need has an experience/design dimension alongside AI, default to considering a joint Bound pitch rather than AiD-solo or an external design partner
- **Commercial model**: Worth formalising — who leads, how margin is split on joint work, how do we position the pair to clients? Not currently captured; ask Will when the moment is right
- **Cross-group (Omnia)**: Omnia owns Bound. If there are other Omnia operating cos, partnership may extend further across the group. Worth learning when next in a Bound conversation.
