# AI Studio — AiD Service Offering

## Overview

The AI Studio is a 4-hour facilitated workshop (also called "AI Blueprint Workshop") designed to move organisations from AI intent to practical, structured action. It combines strategic AI grounding, service design methodology, and applied use case design to create clarity, alignment, and a prioritised roadmap for AI adoption.

The session leverages secure AI tooling within the workshop as an interactive component to bring the use of AI to life.

## Three Core Outcomes (Will's Framing)

1. **AI Literacy Baseline** — Strategic AI grounding covering fundamentals, generative AI, agentic AI in business context, risks, governance, and responsible AI guardrails. Establishes a shared language and common understanding across the leadership team.

2. **Value Identification, Alignment & Prioritisation** — Structured use case ideation using AI Use Case Canvas exercises. Covers group-based future-state ideation, high-level design across business domains, "what would have to be true" activities, and value vs feasibility prioritisation. Co-creation of AI Impact Priorities.

3. **Roadmapping** — Future-state roadmap facilitated discussion to map and sequence AI solutions and identified capabilities. Includes an AI Readiness Snapshot assessing maturity across governance, data, tools, oversight, and capability. Results in a practical, phased Next-Step Roadmap.

## What It Leads To (Follow-on Engagements)

- **AI Literacy Programs** — Deeper capability building based on baseline gaps identified
- **Further Solutioning & PoC of Quick Wins** — Detailed process and use case design, technical solution design for prioritised use cases
- **Investment Cases & Strategic Sequencing** — Business cases for AI initiatives, strategic sequencing of the roadmap

## Delivery Structure (Six Modules)

1. AI Grounding & Strategic Context
2. Real-World Applications & Inspiration (lightning talks, case studies)
3. AI Principles Definition (impact priorities, adoption principles, risk guardrails)
4. AI Use Case Ideation & Prioritisation (Use Case Canvas, future-state design, value vs feasibility)
5. Readiness Assessment (maturity snapshot, priority enablers)
6. Future-State Roadmap

## Scope

**Included:**
- Pre-work alignment session (challenge statement and objectives)
- Review of relevant journey maps or strategic documentation
- Facilitation of 4-hour in-person workshop
- AI Use Case Canvas templates and prioritisation tools
- AI Readiness Snapshot assessment
- Post-workshop summary report (vision, use cases, roadmap)

**Out of Scope:**
- Detailed process and use case design
- Technical solution design of use cases
