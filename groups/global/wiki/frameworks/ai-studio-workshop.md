# AI Studio — AiD's Repeatable Workshop Offering

*Also referred to as the "AI Blueprint Workshop". AiD's foundational service offering.*

## Purpose

A 4-hour facilitated workshop that moves an organisation from *AI intent* to *prioritised action*. Combines strategic AI grounding, service-design methodology, and applied use-case design to produce clarity, leadership alignment, and a phased roadmap for AI adoption.

Secure AI tooling is used live inside the workshop so participants experience agentic capability rather than only hear about it.

## Three core outcomes

1. **AI literacy baseline** — Strategic grounding: generative AI and agentic AI in a business context, risks, governance, responsible-AI guardrails. Establishes shared language and understanding across the leadership team.
2. **Value identification, alignment & prioritisation** — Structured use-case ideation via an AI Use Case Canvas: future-state ideation, high-level design across business domains, "what would have to be true" activities, and value-vs-feasibility prioritisation. Co-creation of AI Impact Priorities.
3. **Roadmapping** — Facilitated discussion to sequence AI solutions and capabilities, including an AI Readiness Snapshot (governance, data, tools, oversight, capability) producing a practical phased next-step roadmap.

## Six delivery modules

1. AI Grounding & Strategic Context
2. Real-World Applications & Inspiration (lightning talks, case studies)
3. AI Principles Definition (impact priorities, adoption principles, risk guardrails)
4. AI Use Case Ideation & Prioritisation (Use Case Canvas, future-state design, value vs feasibility)
5. Readiness Assessment (maturity snapshot, priority enablers)
6. Future-State Roadmap

## Scope

**Included:**
- Pre-work alignment session (challenge statement and objectives)
- Review of relevant journey maps or strategic documentation
- Facilitation of the 4-hour in-person workshop
- AI Use Case Canvas templates and prioritisation tools
- AI Readiness Snapshot assessment
- Post-workshop summary report (vision, use cases, roadmap)

**Out of scope:**
- Detailed process and use-case design
- Technical solution design of individual use cases

## Typical follow-on engagements

- **AI literacy programs** — deeper capability building against gaps identified in the baseline
- **Further solutioning / PoC of quick wins** — detailed process design and technical solution design for prioritised use cases
- **Investment cases & strategic sequencing** — business cases for AI initiatives, phased sequencing of the roadmap

## Commercial shape

- Fixed-fee offering — typically delivered as a single bundled engagement
- Invoiced on delivery of the post-workshop report
- Travel billed at cost

## When to use

- First engagement with a prospective client where AI intent is present but direction is unclear
- Leadership team needs shared grounding before scoping investment
- Organisation wants a practical roadmap, not another strategy document

## Reference implementations

- Workshop has been delivered to a superannuation client (March 2026) and repeatedly referenced as AiD's flagship entry-point service.
